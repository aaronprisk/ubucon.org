UbuCons bring the global Ubuntu community together!

An Ubuntu Conference (often abbreviated to "UbuCon") is a volunteer run, regional event that enables the global community of Ubuntu teams, users, and organizations to come together to work on current challenges, share knowledge, and network within the Ubuntu project.
